The manuscript available for review is an important issue that the authors have presented
and is an imminent public health issue.
However, authors have presented ore of a general view about safe & sustainable
management of self care waste. The idea needs to be presented clearly and should be
backed up by appropriate evidence wherever available.
1. Authors need to define the concept of planetary health as it implies impact not only
human health but also affects the immediate environment and also the animal health.
2. This is an important area of investigation and needs a comprehensive review of existing
literature.
3. Authors should focus on factors affecting self-care products choices and disposal along
with hospital waste which has been presented more generically.
4. At many places in the manuscripts, authors have referred to studied but have not
provided the references.
