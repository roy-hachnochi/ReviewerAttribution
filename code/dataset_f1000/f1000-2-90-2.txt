This is an intriguing report but the approach is a bit simplistic. Atypical presentations of common conditions are more frequently encountered than typical presentations of rare ones. Thus it is really hard to test the hypothesis fully. The internet is more likely to throw up rare options that may result in unnecessary and perhaps dangerous and costly investigations. The fellows are on a journey of understanding and without the experience are likely to score slightly less well than Dr Google. The authors have also made many comparisons and do not appear to have adjusted the level of statistical significance.
