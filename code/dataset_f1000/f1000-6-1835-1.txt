The updated mtCOI reference dataset for the Bemisia tabaci species complex will add a valuable contribution to researches for a fast and accurate identification of members from the B. tabaci species complex based on the partial mitochondrial COI gene. The high quality data is easily accessible for download and gathers whiteflies collected globally. The availability of a reliable and updated reference dataset is an essential tool that will aid the scientific community to identify and classify correctly this pest, the first step in the crop management against whiteflies.
