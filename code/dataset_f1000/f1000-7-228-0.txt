It is an interesting and well-presented case report of pauci-symptomatic, previously overlooked Proteus syndrome in a 49-year-old woman. Proteus syndrome is a rare, complex disorder with multisystem involvement and remarkable clinical variability. The authors highlight the relevance of the recognition of minor cutaneous clinical signs for the diagnosis of minimal forms of the disease, as several complications, some of which life-threatening, may potentially occur in these patients.
