This case report enumerates the dermoscopic findings of oral lichen planus, which is described less in literature. The images, descriptions of each dermoscopic finding, histopathological correlation and discussion is aptly written. Wickham striae seen as veil-like structureless grey-white to bluish white areas, and specked-pearly pattern are highlighting dermoscopic features described here , for oral lichen planus. These findings need to be validated on larger studies.
