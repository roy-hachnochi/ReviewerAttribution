The authors have well prepared the manuscript dealing with critical information about necessary for construction of database of nutrient use efficiency-related genes (NtUE) in cereals and model plants. The authors suggested that this is first of kind to provide comprehensive information dealing with NtUE. The authors have compared also with the known database Gramene which is widely used and suggested the advantage in providing more information of model plants apart from cereals where Gramene only stand. The overall conclusion is the paper is well researched and needs to be indexed.
