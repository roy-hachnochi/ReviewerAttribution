Bronchogenic cyst diagnosis on EBUS and complication of Infection. Needs to alert that Needling with EBUS needle is a recognised complication and some practitioners caution against using EBUS needle if diagnosis can be secured by other means but sometime if there is uncertainty about the diagnosis one has to do the EBUS needling. One has to understand the potential chance of recurrence in absence of complete removal of cyst wall after surgery and possibly need mentioning.
