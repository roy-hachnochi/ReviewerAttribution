This short report aimed to assess the gaps in routine VL monitoring at six months for children (0-9 years) and adolescents (10-19 years) newly initiated on anti-retroviral therapy from Jan 2017 to Sep 2018. This study is essential as such data is needed to assess how programs are fairing with regards to the UNAIDS 90-90-90 target. The study was succinctly reported. All the essentials results based on their study objective were addressed. The study should be accepted.
