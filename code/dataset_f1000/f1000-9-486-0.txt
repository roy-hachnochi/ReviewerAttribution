The main purpose of this paper tries to investigate whether essential oils have any positive or negative effects on growth performance, such as dry matter intake (DMI), average daily gain (ADG), and feed conversion ratio (FCR). The results shown that the essential oils provided for sheep and goats in 12 studies. Most experiments shown the essential oils can improve the above performance. The present study used the Begg's and Egger's tests which were meta-analysis procedures. Please indicate the original words and then can put abbreviations.
