Dengue is now ranking as one of the most critical global mosquito-borne viral diseases and is endemic in over 100 countries. For many countries, dengue is becoming a threat to their public health, and further adversely impacting their health services and economies. Early diagnosis and effective supporting treatment for dengue can reduce transmission and improve patient prognosis. Data on death, delayed treatment are rare.
