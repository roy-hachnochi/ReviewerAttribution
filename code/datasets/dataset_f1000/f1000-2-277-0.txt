This article reported a case of a 14-year old male, diagnosed with CMI who underwent PTA followed by stent placement with immediate positive results. The history was reported clearly, and the procedure was described in detail. The authors also pointed out their experiences concerning the tips of the procedure, such as choosing the right radial artery as the approach to overcome the acute angle of the celiac artery, and the use of nitroglycerine to prevent radial artery vasospasm. Generally, this is an interesting article, and has certain merits for indexing.
