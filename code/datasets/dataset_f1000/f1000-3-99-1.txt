This is a good case report. It highlights the fact that urethral duplication has varied presentations. Ventral divertculum actually helped in diagnosing the patient early as it was a symptomatic cosmetic presentation. The authors are highly skilled in Endourology and skillfully performed the cauterization of the dorsal urethra. This suggests that minimally invasive endourological technique can be used for treatment of duplication. The follow-up MCU shows excellent result.
