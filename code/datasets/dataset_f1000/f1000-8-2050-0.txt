The article is interesting because, beyond the description of the case which is infrequent in itself, it offers interesting hints about ergonomic interventions that can be useful to improve the employment conditions of those suffering from this disease. Since the pathology is progressive and the worker will also undergo periodic checks at the workplace in the future, the authors could say whether, from a total worker health perspective, they have planned interventions aimed at increasing physical activity, avoiding weight accumulation, improve joint mobilisation.
