This Case Report is very much pertinent to the practice of cardiothoracic surgeons treating constrictive pericarditis. Primary Malignant Pericardial Mesothelioma is a rare cause of constrictive pericarditis, but associated with poor surgical outcome, hence it shall be understood and diagnosed well. This case is adequately described and presented appropriately to guide the diagnosing cardiologists and treating cardiothoracic surgeons.
